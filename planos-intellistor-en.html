Functional modules

Each module delivers a specific layer of value to storage operations: governance, automation, protection, FinOps, and intelligence. The composition can evolve as the customer increases operational maturity, criticality, and scope.

Intellistor Foundation

Infrastructure governance
  • Intellistor Platform CMDB
  • Asset visualization
  • Basic status control
  • Basic logs and auditing
  • Integrated technical documentation
  • Local ticket opening

Solution value:
Creates a single source of truth for assets, documentation, and auditing, reducing dependency on spreadsheets and scattered operational knowledge.

Intellistor Operations

Operational automation
  • LUN allocation
  • Hostgroup creation
  • Volume mapping
  • Multivendor automation
  • Standardized operational workflows
  • Pre-execution validations
  • Execution history
  • Vendor-based templates

Solution value:
Reduces manual effort, standardizes execution, and lowers operational risk in critical storage tasks.

Intellistor Data Protection

Data protection
  • Backup environment inventory
  • Backup services
  • Backup policies
  • Job queries
  • Protection status
  • Operational reports
  • Integration with Veritas and future vendors

Solution value:
Provides visibility into protection, policies, and jobs, helping reduce unnoticed failures and improve operational continuity.

Intellistor FinOps

Capacity FinOps
  • FinOps dashboard by vendor
  • Allocated capacity versus physical usage
  • View by controller, hostgroup, and LUN
  • Cost by GB, environment, area, or system
  • Executive reports
  • Showback for financial transparency

Solution value:
Converts technical consumption into financial visibility, supporting showback, capacity optimization, and investment decisions.

Intellistor Intelligence

Predictive intelligence and recommendations
  • Predictive capacity analysis
  • Optimization recommendations
  • Anomaly detection
  • Right-sizing suggestions
  • Operational assistant
  • Insight generation
  • Risk prioritization
  • Trend analysis

Solution value:
Anticipates risks, identifies trends, and recommends actions before capacity, performance, or operations become a problem.
